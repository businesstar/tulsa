Common Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most screen and battery repairs are completed in 15-30 minutes. Water damage diagnostics take 24-48 hours. We work efficiently while maintaining quality standards.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes! Every repair comes with our lifetime warranty covering both parts and labor. If anything goes wrong with the repair we performed, we'll fix it free of charge.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome! However, booking ahead ensures we have the specific part for your model in stock and can minimize your wait time.

Will I lose my data?

Most repairs (screens, batteries, ports) don't affect your data. For water damage or motherboard repairs, we recommend backing up if possible. We offer data recovery services for non-functional devices.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Tulsa and surrounding areas including Broken Arrow, Bixby, Jenks, Owasso, and Sand Springs. Mobile repairs available for select issues.
